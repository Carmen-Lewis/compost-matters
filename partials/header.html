Neighbourhood Composting

Turning Food Scraps into Soil

The Sybrand Park Composting Initiative aims to make our suburb a Food Waste Free Zone by creating a community composting space. Residents drop off food scraps; together we turn them into compost for our neighbourhood gardens.
