Volunteers’ Section

There are three easy ways to help:

  • Compost Champion: Do short drop-off demos and answer quick questions.
  • Bin-Opening Rota: Join the weekly rotation to open/close and check the bins.
  • Light Maintenance: Help with turning, topping with browns, sweeping, and simple record-keeping.

Ready to jump in? WhatsApp us to join the Sybrand Park Composting Initiative.
